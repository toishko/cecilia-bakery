Payment

Payment is collected securely online at the time of placing your order. We accept all major credit and debit cards, processed through our payment partner, Clover.

Cancellations & Refunds

We understand plans change. If you need to cancel an order, please keep the following in mind:

  • Custom cake orders are non-refundable once preparation has started, as these items are made specifically to your request

If we are unable to fulfill your order for any reason, you will receive a full refund.
